Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before you begin.

How does Klaropoint fit into existing workflows?

Klaropoint is designed to add structured validation, reconciliation, and documentation around existing clinic processes rather than forcing an immediate replacement of core systems.

Do we need to replace our EHR?

No. Klaropoint is designed to work alongside existing clinical, billing, clearinghouse, and payer workflows.

How is information protected?

Protection includes encrypted transport and storage, scoped access, logical separation, controlled processing, and activity records. Detailed controls are reviewed during security evaluation.

How long does onboarding take?

Timing depends on data sources, integration scope, security review, and clinic readiness. A focused initial assessment establishes the expected path and timeline.

Can we review contractual and compliance requirements?

Yes. Relevant security, privacy, data handling, and contractual requirements are reviewed before sensitive information is exchanged.
