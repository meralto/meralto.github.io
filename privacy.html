Website privacy notice

Privacy and responsible information handling.

Draft for legal review: This notice should be reviewed and adapted by qualified counsel before public launch.

Information collected

When you submit a website form, Klaropoint may receive your name, work email, organization, role, inquiry type, and the message you provide.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to your inquiry, evaluate your request, maintain business records, protect the website, and improve communications.

Form processor

The contact form uses Formspree as an independent form-processing provider. Form submissions are transmitted to Formspree and then delivered to Klaropoint. Review Formspree’s terms, privacy practices, retention settings, and account security before launch.

No patient information

Do not submit patient names, medical record numbers, claims, diagnoses, payment files, PHI, or other sensitive clinical information through this website.

Retention and disclosure

Business inquiries may be retained as needed for follow-up, security, legal, and operational purposes. Information may be disclosed to service providers that support website and communication operations, subject to appropriate review.

Your choices

You may request correction or deletion of website inquiry information by contacting privacy@klaropoint.com, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

Updates

This notice may be updated as website practices and legal requirements change. Add an effective date after legal approval.
